Refactor and polish this project as a production-ready SaaS called Pillar.

Brand / positioning
- Rename the product from Steward to Pillar everywhere in the app
- Domain references should use mypillar.co
- Primary tagline: “Your organization, on autopilot.”
- Category/positioning: software for civic organizations, nonprofits, lodges, HOAs, PTAs, unions, religious groups, and community organizations
- Do not overemphasize AI in the UI copy. Position AI as a behind-the-scenes capability, not the main selling point
- Overall tone: trustworthy, modern, professional, calm, not hypey

Product positioning
- Pillar is not a generic AI company
- Pillar is an operations platform for volunteer-run organizations
- The strongest value proposition is:
  - Website
  - Events
  - Communications
  - Board approvals
  - Contacts
  - Sponsors/vendors
  in one place
- Make continuity and reduced chaos a major theme throughout the product and landing page

Pricing
- Keep these four tiers and present them clearly:
  - Starter: $29/mo or $24/mo annually
  - Autopilot: $59/mo or $49/mo annually
  - Events: $99/mo or $84/mo annually
  - Total Operations: $149/mo or $124/mo annually
- 14-day free trial on all tiers
- 2.5% platform fee on ticket sales for Events and Total Operations
- Custom domain add-on: $24/year for Starter, included on higher tiers
- Highlight Autopilot as most popular
- Pricing copy should emphasize flat, predictable monthly pricing with no surprises
